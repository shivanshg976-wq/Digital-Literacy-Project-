What is Digital Literacy?

Digital literacy is often misunderstood as simply knowing how to turn on a computer, use a smartphone, or scroll through social media. However, it is much deeper than that. Digital literacy is the ability to confidently find, evaluate, create, and communicate information safely and responsibly in the digital world. Think of it like traditional literacy (reading and writing). Just as you need to know how to read a book and write a letter to function in the physical world, you need digital literacy to function effectively in the modern, technology-driven world.
