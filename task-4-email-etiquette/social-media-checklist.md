Do’s:

1. Do assume everything you post is public and permanent, even on private accounts.

2. Do use privacy settings to control who can see your personal updates and location.

3. Do separate your professional profiles (like LinkedIn) from your casual personal profiles (like Instagram).

4. Do engage respectfully with others and think before commenting on controversial topics.

5. Do verify information before sharing or retweeting to avoid spreading fake news.

Don’ts:

1. Don't share sensitive personal information like your home address, phone number, or OTPs.

2. Don't post negative comments or complaints about your college, professors, or future employers.

3. Don't engage with internet trolls or participate in cyberbullying.

4. Don't use offensive language, slurs, or inappropriate humor that could damage your digital footprint.

5. Don't accept friend requests or messages from completely unknown or suspicious accounts.
